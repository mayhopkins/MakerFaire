Bentley Maker Faire

February 7, 2020

This is Bentley's third annual Maker Faire. The Maker Faire is a great opportunity to explore cool math concepts.

The Maker Faire is important because it connects math to real-world problems.

Here are some of the projects that students choose to create:

  • Designs on Desmos
  • Making Catapults and Calculating Parabolas
  • Projects using the Fibonacci Sequence
  • Statistic Projects based on Probability
